Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the moon sometimes out during the day?

+

The moon is visible during the day because it orbits the Earth and can be above the horizon during daylight hours.

Why is the sky blue?

+

The sky is blue due to the scattering of sunlight by the atmosphere. The blue light is scattered in all directions, making the sky appear blue.
